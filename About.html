About the Company

Founded in 2019 by two brothers who were Professional Drivers for Uber & Lyft at this time and decided to venture out into business for themselves.
Utilizing the experience gained through Gig-Economy work throughout 2018-2019, they started G2L Transport and Rideshare to address some of the issues with transportation throughout the valley and to improve the transportation experience for the state of Arizona and the City of Phoenix and all surrounding areas.
